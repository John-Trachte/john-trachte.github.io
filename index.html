    Some Projects

    One of the coolest electrical phenomena (in my opinion) is that the frequency of an oscillation between two voltages tracks directly to the same frequency in a sound wave - it's too convenient not to dive headlong into electrical instrument design, from power delivery to frequency generation to pitch control.

    I guess it follows that I've been working on my own synthesizer design, in tandem with what I've been learning in class. So far, I have built and tested the basics of a Voltage Controlled Oscillator, with a simple and cost-effective bill of materials that lets me deploy as many as I need.
